About MemoTrack

MemoTrack is a hospital workflow management platform designed to simplify maintenance reporting and operational communication in healthcare facilities.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve hospital infrastructure management by providing a reliable digital system for tracking maintenance issues and operational tasks.

The Problem We Solve

Hospitals often rely on manual communication to report maintenance issues. This can cause delays, missed requests, and poor tracking.

MemoTrack solves this by providing a centralized issue tracking system accessible through web and mobile applications.
