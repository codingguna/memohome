Digital Issue Reporting in Hospitals

Hospitals experience a variety of infrastructure issues such as equipment malfunction, electrical faults, plumbing problems, and sanitation concerns.

Traditional reporting methods often make it difficult to ensure that these issues are tracked and resolved efficiently.

Centralized Issue Tracking

Digital platforms provide centralized dashboards where staff can report problems and administrators can monitor the status of each request.

Improved Accountability

Each issue is recorded and assigned to responsible teams, ensuring accountability and faster resolution.
